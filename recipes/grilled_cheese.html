Grilled cheese recipe

Description

A delicious grilled cheese that probably wasn't really made on a grill. They're kind of a huge scam that way

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat non-stick pan to medium temperature.
  2. Cut enough cheese to cover bread all the way to all four corners.
  3. Butter one side of two pieces of bread.
  4. Put one piece of bread butter side down onto hot pan, add cheese, top with remaining piece of bread buttered side up.
  5. Cook until lightly golden brown and flip to uncooked side. Flip frequently after lightly golden until all cheese is melted.
