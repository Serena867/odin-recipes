Bread

Description

A loaf of white bread perfect for sandwiches

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Canola oil
  • sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add warm water, yeast, sugar, canola oil, and salt to a bowl. Whisk until frothy.
  2. Add 3/4 of the flour, mix thoroughly with your hands. When mixed add the remaining flour and mix.
  3. Cover with damp kitchen towel and allow to rise for one hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Once dough has risen coat a bread pan in oil or butter.
  5. Knead the bread dough for 2 to 3 minutes and place in bread pan.
  6. Allow dough to rise in bread pan until it has nearly doubled. At this point heat oven to 375f
  7. Once bread has doubled in size in pan place in oven for 30 to 35 minutes until golden brown on top.
